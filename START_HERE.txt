================================================================================
                           COMMON QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Where do I find these books on MAM?
A: Go to https://www.myanonamouse.net/tor/search.php and search for the title

Q: What if a book isn't available?
A: Skip it and come back later. Try the next one.

Q: How do I add torrents to qBittorrent?
A: Download .torrent file, then open with qBittorrent

Q: When will books appear in Audiobookshelf?
A: Usually within 24 hours after torrent finishes downloading

Q: What's the ratio thing everyone talks about?
A: Ratio = Upload/Download. Keep it above 1.0 by seeding your torrents

Q: How long does a download take?
A: Depends on seeders. Could be hours or days. Patience needed.

Q: Can I download multiple books at once?
A: Yes, but don't overwhelm your internet. Start with 2-3 at a time.

Q: Do I need to do anything special for Audiobookshelf?
A: Once files download to your books folder, Audiobookshelf scans them
